Super Mario 64

Super Mario 64 is a game developed by Nintendo and released on the Nintendo 64 console. In the game, you (Mario) explore Peach's castle and save her from Bowser. The game is open-world, allowing movement through all three axes. It was first released in Japan on June 23, 1996, and then later released in North America, Europe, and Australia.

Super Mario Sunshine

Super Mario Sunshine is a Gamecube game created by Nintendo. In the game, Shadow Mario, a villain look-alike of Mario, vandalizes the island, and you (Mario) get framed. Mario then has to clean the island by going through levels and using the Flash Liquidizer Ultra Dousing Device, also known as the F.L.U.D.D., in order to save Princess Peach and free your name. It was released in Japan on July 19, 2002, and then in North America, Europe, and Australia afterwards.

Super Mario Galaxy

Super Mario Galaxy is a Nintendo game for the Wii. In the game, you explore different galaxies and planets, with varying enemies, power-ups, and gravity, to accomplish your goal of rescuing Princess Peach and defeating Bowser. It was originally released in 2007, but re-released for different consoles in 2011 and 2015.

Super Mario Galaxy 2

Super Mario Galaxy 2 is a platforming Wii game made by Nintendo. The game involves collecting power stars to fuel your ship, so you can travel to the center of the universe and rescue Peach. While it's the sequel of Super Mario Galaxy, it was originally planned to be an update to it. It brings many new additions, including new power-ups, new enemies, new levels, and Yoshi. It was announced in 2009, and released worldwide in 2010, 3 years after the original.



Super Mario 3D Land

Super Mario 3D Land is a Nintendo 3DS game developed by Nintendo. This game is unique because it takes elements from 2D side-scrolling games in the Mario series and from 3D free-roam Mario games. In the game, Princess Peach has been kidnapped by Bowser (again), and Mario must rescue her. The game released worldwide in 2011.



Super Mario 3D World

Super Mario 3D World is a Wii U game created by Nintendo. In the game, Mario and friends have to rescue Sprixies that Bowser kidnapped. The gameplay is similiar to Super Mario 3D Land, since Super Mario 3D World is its sequel. It introduces a new power-up called the Super Bell, which transforms you into a cat. It was released in 2013.

Super Mario Odyssey

Super Mario Odyssey is a Nintendo game for the Nintendo Switch. The game follows Mario and Cappy, a hat spirit that turned into Mario's hat, allowing Mario to possess characters and objects. The game follows their journey across different kingdoms to save Peach and Cappy's sister. The game is open-world, similar to Super Mario 64 and Super Mario Sunshine. The game was released worldwide on October 27, 2017.
